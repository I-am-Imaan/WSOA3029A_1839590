I wanted to create a website that is quite minimalistic in it's design. I have made use of consistent aspects and elements within my website, in order for users to understand and learn how to use the websites interface much easier. The content displayed on each web-page is intended to be minimalistic and only provide the most needed information, while still being appealing. Most of my content is left-aligned, in order to make it more readable for users. I have made use of a sticky navbar, which allows for users to have access to the different webpages, no matter their scroll position. I have also included a scroll to top button for ease of use.
