Styleguide

I wanted to create a website colour palette that consisted of colours usually associated with a sense of calm. My website deals with and addressses a topic that is quite serious, but I did not want to create an intense atmosphere and rather allow users to feel at ease and more comfortable when browsing the website and learning about diabetes. I have designed my logo in accordance with the website name "Diabetic Drop", and it consists of a lower case letter 'd' within a droplet - making use of both the sites main colours. I have chosen to use a combination of the fonts 'Montserrat', as well as 'Poppins' as they work well together and are readable.
