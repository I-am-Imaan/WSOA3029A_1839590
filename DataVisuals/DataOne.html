How Blood Glucose Levels Are Influenced

There are number of things that impact the blood glucose levels of a diabetic - not only sugar consumption. Eating increases blood glucose levels in general, but the higher the glycaemic index of the food type, the more the increase in sugar levels. Sugars and carbs increase blood glucose levels most, while fruits, veggies, dairy and fats increase blood glucose levels to a lesser extent - with proteins increasing them the least. Other factors, such as stress, lack of sleep and low body temperatures also increase blood glucose levels as they are not good for a diabetic's health and wellbeing. More sleep, higher body temperatures, and enough hydration decrease blood glucose levels. Taking Insulin and exercising, decrease blood glucose levels most.
